What do we do?

Get to know us!

Welcome to the home page of the Los Altos Hills Youth Commission. The LAHYC is a group of twenty Los Altos Hills Teens who seek to enfranchise the youth of the area by providing them with an opportunity to connect with one another.

What's going on?

Visit Our Blog!

We regularly host events to foster interaction and build connections. These free events allow middle school and high school students from different schools to interact. In addition, you can find us at Los Altos Hills town events such as the Hoedown, Barn Lighting, Earth Day Celebration, Pathways Run, and the Town Picnic.

How do I join?

Application and Contact Information

Over the course of the year, the Los Altos Hills Youth Commission holds 3 main events. We plan the Leadership Conference and the Movie Night in the spring semester. We also hold an annual Middle School Dodgeball tournament with the Los Altos Youth Comission.
